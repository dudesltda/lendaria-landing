**Brand essentials at a glance:**
- Four colours, in this order: Amarelo Lendário `#F6C324` (the torch), Preto `#191919` (the void), Branco Marfim `#FFFFF0` (paper), Bege Lendário `#F2EBDA` (sand / editorial). No gradients. No tints. No fifth colour.
- Type: Archivo Black (display), Archivo (UI/body), JetBrains Mono (data labels).
- Square brackets `[ ]` are a structural motif: `[IA]`, `[MÓDULO 03]`, `[LENDÁRIO]`.
- Symbols: `∞` (infinito), torch flame, brain — use sparingly, earn the moment.
- Voice: manifesto (poetic, sweeping) for brand surfaces; wizard (enigmatic, technical, modest) for marketing/product. "Você" always. Brazilian PT-BR; English available for international.
- Zero corner radius. Hard 8px offset solid shadows only. Hover = invert fill. Press = translate down-right + collapse shadow.
